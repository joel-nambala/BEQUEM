About us

Our history

As a division of the Bequem Group, Bequem Records Management is an integral part of its history, and has become an increasingly important player in the global records management industry.

Our expertise

Our solutions are designed to facilitate the management of your documents. We know how important your records are to your business and we are proud to be part of their conservation.

Quality, confidential and security

We understand how important your records are and take care of them as if they were our own. Our procedures are constantly evolving, which allows us to stay at the top of the industry while providing you with the best document storage services.

Corporate social responsibility

Bequem Records Management works every day for the greater good of the planet and its citizens. Bequem Records Management is involved in several projects that uphold the values of quality, performance, innovation, respect, and solidarity.
